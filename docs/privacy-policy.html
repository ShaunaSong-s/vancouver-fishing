Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Top Vancouver Fishing Charter Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Fishing Assistant mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

We collect your device's location data only when you are actively using the App (when-in-use permission). Location data is used to:

  • Display your position on the marine map
  • Recommend nearby fishing spots
  • Calculate marine navigation routes
  • Provide localized weather and tide information

Your location data is processed on-device and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

AI Chat Data

When you use the AI fishing assistant feature, your messages are sent to a third-party AI service (Groq/OpenAI) for processing. We do not store chat history on our servers. Chat history is stored locally on your device only.

User Preferences

App settings (language preference, captain mode, custom API keys) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not collect financial or payment information
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Open-Meteo API — Weather and marine forecast data (no personal data sent)
  • DFO IWLS API — Canadian tide and water level data (no personal data sent)
  • Groq AI API — AI chat processing (chat messages only, no location or personal data)
  • Apple MapKit — Map display (subject to Apple's privacy policy)

4. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App removes all stored data. We maintain no server-side user data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security is maintained through iOS's built-in data protection mechanisms. Custom API keys you enter are stored in iOS UserDefaults on-device only.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to delete, export, or modify. You can clear all local App data by uninstalling the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App and on this page with an updated revision date.
