## Description (English)

Your ultimate fishing companion for Vancouver's Georgia Strait! Get real-time marine weather, discover top fishing spots, plan safe routes, and chat with an AI fishing expert — all in one app.

MARINE WEATHER MAP
• Real-time wind, wave, temperature, pressure overlays
• Choose from 4 weather models: GEM, GFS, ECMWF, ICON
• PredictWind-quality visualization with smooth color gradients
• 24-hour forecast graphs for any point on the map
• Wind particles animation showing real-time flow

FISHING SPOTS
• Curated spots across Georgia Strait, Howe Sound, and Fraser River
• Species info: Chinook, Coho, Pink, Sockeye, Halibut, Lingcod, Crab
• Seasonal recommendations and gear/bait suggestions
• DFO regulations and conservation closures

ROUTE PLANNER (Captain Mode)
• Tap-to-add waypoint navigation
• Real-time weather along your route
• Distance and heading calculations
• Safety alerts for wind and wave conditions

AI FISHING ASSISTANT
• Chat with "渔友" — your AI fishing buddy
• Get species-specific tackle advice
• Ask about regulations, tides, and best times to fish
• Works offline with cached responses

TIDES & CURRENTS
• DFO official tide predictions for BC stations
• High/low tide times and heights
• Tidal current forecasts

INFORMATION CENTER
• DFO fishing regulations reference
• Spot Prawn and shellfish season dates
• Boat fleet showcase
• Fishing guide profiles

Built by Vancouver fishermen, for Vancouver fishermen. Safe trips, tight lines! 🎣

## App Review Notes

This app provides fishing-related information for the Vancouver, British Columbia area. Key features to test:

1. Marine Map: Open the app → first tab shows marine weather map. Tap overlay buttons to switch between Wind/Wave/Temperature modes.
2. AI Assistant: Go to "Info" tab → tap "AI Assistant" to chat. Ask "what fish are in season?"
3. Captain Mode: Go to "Info" tab → Settings → enable "I'm a Captain" to unlock Route Planner tab.
4. Location: The app requests location permission to show your position on the marine map. Denying location still allows the app to function.

Demo account: No login required. All features work without authentication.
